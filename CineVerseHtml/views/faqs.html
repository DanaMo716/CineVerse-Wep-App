Frequently Asked Questions

What is CineVerse?
Personalized movie rating platform designed to help you keep track of and rate the movies you've watched
How do I sign up?
Signing up for CineVerse is easy! Simply click on the "Sign up" button on the homepage, and follow the prompts to connect your Google account. Once connected, you can start rating and reviewing movies right away.
Is it free to use?
Yes, CineVerse is completely free to use. There are no subscription fees or hidden charges. Our goal is to provide an accessible platform for movie lovers to share their thoughts and recommendations.
Can I add new movies?
Yes, you can add any movie that you want to add just click on the "Add new movie" button and write the movie information and your rate.
